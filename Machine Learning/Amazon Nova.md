# 1. Introduction

- Family of AI Foundation Models (FMs) built by AWS.
- Think of it as AWS' alternative to ChatGPT or Claude.
- Designed to be fast, cost-effective, enterprise-ready.
- Accessible through Amazon Bedrock.
  ![Amazon Novaa](/Images/Machine%20Learning/AmazonNova.png)

# 2. Models

- **Understanding**
  - **Nova Premier:** The most capable of Amazon's multimodal models for complex reasoning tasks and for use as the best teacher for distilling custom models.
  - **Nova Pro:** A highly capable multimodal model with the best combination of accuracy, speed, and cost for a wide range of tasks.
  - **Nova Lite:** A very low-cost multimodal model that is lightning fast for processing image, video, and text inputs.
  - **Nova Micro:** A text-only model that delivers the lowest latency responses at very low cost.
- **Creative**
  - **Nova Canvas:** A state-of-the-art image generation model.
  - **Nova Reel:** A state-of-the-art video generation model.
- **Speech**
  - **Nova Sonic:** Conversational speech understanding and generation in multiple languages.

# 3. Amazon Nova 2

- New Models and enhanced capabilities to build complex AI apps.
- Interactive chatbots, analyze documents and videos, create AI agents...
- Up to 1M tokens of context and advanced reasoning capabilities.
- **Nova 2 Lite:** Fast, cost-effective reasoning model for everyday workloads (text, images, videos, documents)
- **Nova 2 Sonic:** Speech-to-speech FM that brings natural, real-time voice conversations to our apps (speech, text).
- **Nova 2 Multimodal Embeddings:** State-of-the-art multimodal embedding model for agentic RAG and semantic search apps.
- **Nova 2 Omni:** All-in-one model for multimodal reasoning and image generation.
